About me

Clarity, impact,
iteration.

I'm Barbara — a UX and Product Designer who loves solving problems that actually matter. I'm driven by clarity, impact, and the kind of iteration that turns a good idea into a proven one.

My background sits at the intersection of design, data, and human behaviour. Whether I'm running usability tests in-store, digging into Contentsquare heatmaps, or building out a design system, I'm always asking the same question: what's actually going on for the user here?

I'm trilingual (English, Spanish, Portuguese), detail-obsessed, and genuinely excited by the challenge of making complex things feel simple.
